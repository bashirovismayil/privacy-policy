2. Payment Information

AL&GO does not collect or store payment card details. All payment transactions are securely processed by our partnered payment service providers. We only receive transaction confirmation (such as a digital receipt) necessary to complete your purchase in partnered supermarkets.

We use your personal information to:

We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.
